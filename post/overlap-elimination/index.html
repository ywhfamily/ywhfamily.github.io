Overlap Elimination

2021-02-16

Puzzle 1 + 1 = 0,
Black & White, Tricolor(Red, Green, Blue).
Challenge your brain.

Overlap Elimination is an interesting puzzle game. The objective is moving the pieces to make the same shape showing on board. The rule is: When even number pieces are overlapping, the overlapped part will be eliminated and display background(Black + Black = White). In Tricolor mode, when two different color pieces are overlapping the overlapped part will show another color(Red + Green = Blue).

Player can make the shape at any place on the board. Player also can overlap on the original shape.

Feature:
Nine Packages, 1020 Levels.
Big Play Area, Cover Mode.
